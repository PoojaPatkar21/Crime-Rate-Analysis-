Why crime analysis is done ?

Growing crime rate is a major concern and it is essential to find techniques to reduce it. Manually analysing crime data is not easy or impossible in some cases. Crime analysis gives us better understanding of crime trends which is highly beneficial. There is a necessity for tools that can help us analyze and predict crime rate in order to ensure proper surveillance and reduce crime. Criminal data is available for public but it is mostly focused on combined data about a state. A simple web based tool can help the public to understand about the crime data. By spotting the crime prone areas the general public can be given an alert about the crimes in different parts of a country.

About website!

This website is for analysing and visualizing the crime data in a simple and easy manner for Maharashtra State. User can go throught the crime statistics along with charts, maps, etc. Simple crime maps and statistical data in the form of graphs can help the police as well as the citizens in many ways. The simple graphical representation of the crime data is easily understandable.
The analysis of crime rates for Maharashtra state is very useful in studying if the crime rate is increasing or decreasing in a particular district or region and act accordingly.
